History of Candy Crush Saga:

Candy Crush Saga is a wildly popular match-three puzzle game developed by King. It was first released on April 12, 2012, for Facebook and quickly gained immense popularity.
The game's success led to its expansion onto various platforms, including mobile devices like iOS and Android. Its addictive gameplay, colorful graphics, and clever mechanics made it a global sensation.
Over the years, Candy Crush Saga has continued to evolve with new levels, challenges, and features, keeping players engaged and addicted.

How to play?

Candy Crush Saga is a match-three puzzle game where players need to swap colorful candies to create sets of three or more candies of the same color. The game is played on a grid filled with different types of candies. Here's how to play:

  1. Know Your Tasks
    • Clearing a certain number of candies
    • Achieving a target score
    • Collecting special items
  2. Matching Candies
    • Swap adjacent candies to create matches of three or more candies of the same color
    • Matches can be horizontal or vertical
  3. Creating Special Candies
    • Creating matches of 4+ candies results in special candies with unique abilities
  4. Combining Special Candies
    • Combining special candies for more powerful effects
  5. Obstacles and Challenges
    • As players progress through the levels, they'll encounter various obstacles like chocolate, licorice, and more, which add complexity to the gameplay
  6. Lives and Boosters
    • - Players have a limited number of lives to attempt a level. Boosters and power-ups can be earned or purchased to help overcome difficult levels
